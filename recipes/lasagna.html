Creamy Homemade Hot Cocoa

Cosy up with a hot mug of hot chocolate this winter. This old-fashioned hot chocolate recipe is incredibly rich,creamy, and oh-satisfying

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basic:

There are the simple ingredient you'll need to make the best hot chocolate of your entire life:

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ⅓ cup boiling water
  • 3 ½ cups milk
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup half-and-half cream

steps

  1. step1: Gather the ingredients.
  2. step2: Combine sugar, cocoa powder, and salt in a saucepan; add boiling water and whisk until smooth. Bring mixture to a simmer, stirring continuously to prevent scorching, and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. step3: Stir in milk and heat until very hot without boiling.
  4. step4: Remove from heat; add vanilla.
  5. step5: Add cream to each mug to help cool cocoa to drinking temperature then divide hot cocoa between 4 mugs.
  6. step6: Serve hot and enjoy!
